How It Began

Started with a craving that no store could satisfy

Mystify Me wasn't planned — it was felt. It started with a simple frustration: every chocolate in the market tasted the same. Factory-made, preservative-filled, forgettable.

So we started making our own. In a small Jaipur kitchen, with no machines, no shortcuts, and an obsession for getting it right. Every truffle was hand-poured into its mold. Every box was packed by hand.

People tasted them and kept coming back. That's how Mystify Me was born.
